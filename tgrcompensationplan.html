Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Click on a question to reveal the answer.

What is Top-Up and Get Reward (TGR)?

TGR LTD is a registered Nigerian company (RC: 1505794) offering a VTU (Virtual Top-Up) platform. It allows members to earn cashback on personal usage (Airtime, Data, Cable TV, Electricity) and commissions/bonuses by sharing the platform and building a team.

Who is the CEO?

The CEO is Mr. Adesayo Samuel Babatunde, FCA (Fellow Chartered Accountant).

Is TGR Legitimate and Registered?

Yes, TGR LTD is fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) with RC number 1505794. It is also verified by FIRS and licensed by the NCC (Nigerian Communications Commission).

How much does it cost to join?

Registration itself is free, but your account needs to be activated by choosing a package. Activation packages range from ₦2,500 (Pearl Mini) to ₦100,000 (Executive Diamond).

Do I have to refer people to earn?

No. You can earn cashback (2%-2.5%) simply by using the platform for your own recharges and bill payments, or by selling VTU services to others. However, referring others unlocks significantly higher earning potential through commissions and incentives.

What is PV (Point Value)?

PV is Point Value. You earn PV when new members register in your downline (within the levels eligible for your package). PV accumulates over time and qualifies you for leadership incentives like trips, cars, and house funds.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Yes, you can start with any package and upgrade to a higher one at any time by paying the difference. Upgrading unlocks deeper commission levels and PV earning potential.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your earnings directly to your registered local bank account from your TGR e-wallet. Withdrawals are typically processed quickly (often within minutes). You can also use your earnings to fund your VTU wallet or transfer to other TGR members.

Is there a monthly fee or renewal?

No, the package activation fee is a one-time payment. There are no compulsory monthly fees or renewal costs.
